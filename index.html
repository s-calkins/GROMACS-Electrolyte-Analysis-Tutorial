GROMACS Electrolytic Solution Tutorial

Tutorial Overview

This GROMACS tutorial details the modeling and analysis of an aqueous zinc sulfate (ZnSO₄) electrolyte system. The tutorial will begin with environment configuration and will conclude with processing and data analysis, consisting of a brief investigation of solvation structures through calculating radial distribution functions (RDFs), and various diffusion coefficients through computing the mean squared displacement (MSD) of the ionic species.

This tutorial applies to all GROMACS versions within the 2018.x series.
